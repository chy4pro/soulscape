"Your Life is the Script. Your Heart is the Director."

Welcome to the official information hub for the world's first movement dedicated to Resonant AI Storytelling. We are gathering 200 of the world's most visionary creators for an elite 48-Hour Cinema Lab (Elite Hackthon) to reclaim the soul of cinema in the age of attention economy.

The Event

  • Dates: April 9 - 12, 2026 (3.5 Days)
  • Location: KQED, San Francisco, CA
  • Format: A 48-Hour Cinema Lab where AI Artists form teams, judged & guided by Hollywood legends, and produce a short film from concept to premiere and the top IP made during 48 will be evaluate for global distribution.
  • Website: soulscapefilm.com

The Mission

Current AI content is often "slop"—technically impressive but emotionally hollow. We are changing that.

  • Resonance over Algorithms: We prioritize stories that make us feel.
  • Connection over Competition: This is a relay, not a race. We lift each other up.
  • Ethical Innovation: We champion the responsible, artist-centric use of AI tools.

The Venue: Lab vs. Summit

Make no mistake—the Cinema Lab is a thrilling race against the clock.

The Cinema Lab (Floors 2 & 3): This is your battleground. You will spend most of your time here testing the absolute limits of AI generation to craft a cinema-grade film.
The Summit (Floor 1): The main Summit (panels, masterclasses, and speaker series) happens downstairs. You will have time to socialize and network with Hollywood legends, but your primary mission is upstairs.

(Note: If you do not want to compete and only want to attend Summit panels, please secure a General Ticket at luma.com/lgbhf1my).

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What experience do I need?

No coding required, but prior AI filmmaking experience is mandatory. You will need to submit a portfolio link showcasing your narrative skills as a Storyteller, Director, Editor, or Tech Artist.

Do I need a full team to apply?

No! We accept both Team and Individual applications. Solo creators can build their "Soul Squad" before the event via our #team-formation channel or in-person at the on-site mixer.

Is there an entry fee?

No. Soulscape is committed to creative equity.

Can I participate remotely?

The main Cinema Lab is in-person in SF at KQED to maximize collaboration, but we may have online "Satellite" tracks (Details TBA).

What tools will be provided?

To ensure a powerful and fair production environment, we will provide premium access to canvas tools (Tapnow, YouArt, Pixero) and a designated pool of elite models (Kling, Minimax, Gemini, Sora 2, etc.).
