The Setup: Breakfast & Coffee

Tartine Manufactory 595 Alabama (at 18th) • Local Icon Grab a loaf of the best bread in SF. Great for breakfast too — the babka is incredible.
Sightglass Coffee 3014 20th St • Best in Hood Best coffee nearby. Try the vanilla iced coffee. Leans light roast.

Atlas Cafe 3049 20th St Sandwiches, salads, coffee, and beer. Turkey sandwich or chicken goddess salad.

Southern Pacific Brewing 620 Treat • KQED Staff Fave The neighborhood's go-to for groups. Great craft beer, solid food, tons of outdoor seating.
Farmhouse Thai 710 Florida • Upscale The fried chicken is legendary. Outdoor parklet. The kind of place you take people to impress them.
El Faro 2399 Folsom St • SF Legend Where the first retail burrito in San Francisco was sold — September 26, 1961.
San Ho Won 2170 Bryant St • Dinner Only Esquire Best New Restaurant 2022. Reservation required. Absolutely worth it.
True Laurel Near Alabama St • James Beard Featured on KQED Food's Check, Please! Bay Area S17. A SF institution for cocktails.
