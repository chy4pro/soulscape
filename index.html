Film Sprint Agenda

A carefully designed journey from belief to finished film

Day 1

The Foundation of Thought and the Fusion of Tools

10:00 AM - 1:00 PM
Arrival & "The Belief Archive"
Record the stories and beliefs you want to express through film. AI will instantly generate a "thought filmstrip" that serves as your entry pass.
1:00 PM - 4:00 PM
"Belief Resonance" & Team Formation
Form teams based on shared values and story themes through an informal mixer.
4:00 PM - 6:00 PM
"The Belief Toolkit" Workshop
AI Visual Poetry: Transforming emotions into visuals with prompts. Master the core functions of AI video tools.
7:00 PM - 10:00 PM
Theme Reveal & Script "Engineering"
A "Forum of Belief" where filmmaking masters and AI thought leaders announce the event's theme. Begin prototyping your stories.

Days 2-4

The Belief Engineering Sprint (72 Hours of Uninterrupted Creation)

"This isn't a silent exam. This is a collaborative journey."

All Day
Creative Marathon
The venue transforms into a "Thought Lab," open 24/7 for teams to create. Optional parallel workshops available throughout. Work alongside fellow storytellers, share ideas, and build together in a supportive environment.
Day 2 AM
Narrative Mapping Workshop
How to use AI to structure your story from a human perspective. Learn to craft compelling narratives that resonate with audiences while leveraging AI tools for efficient story development.
Day 2 PM
Emotional Palette Workshop
How to create mood and atmosphere with AI. Master the art of visual storytelling by using AI to generate imagery that captures the emotional core of your narrative.
Day 3
Sound and Soul Workshop
How to use AI to assist with scoring and sound design. Discover how audio can elevate your film's emotional impact and learn to work with AI music generation and sound design tools.
Day 4
Prototype Presentation Workshop
Refine your core message and prepare for the final showcase. Learn storytelling techniques for presenting your work effectively and receive feedback from mentors and peers.

The Cultural Launch

10:00 AM - 12:00 PM

3:00 PM - 6:00 PM

All films will be screened in a professional theater. The "Cultural Prototype Awards" will be presented, honoring works that have successfully "engineered" powerful beliefs into film.
Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need filmmaking experience?
No! This event is designed for everyone with a story to tell, regardless of technical experience. We'll provide workshops to help you master AI filmmaking tools quickly.
What equipment do I need?
Just bring your laptop and your creativity. We'll provide access to AI video tools and all necessary resources for creating your film.
Do I need to have a team?
You can come solo or with a team. We'll have a team formation session on Day 1 where you can connect with others who share your vision and values.
What kind of stories can I tell?
Any story that comes from your heart. Whether it's personal, fictional, documentary-style, or experimental‚Äîif it means something to you, we want to see it.
Is this a competition?
This isn't a competition‚Äîit's a movement. While we'll have awards, our focus is on providing a stage for every storyteller and building a community of creators.
What happens after the event?
Your journey doesn't end here; it's just the beginning. Your film will be showcased to our community, but more importantly, you'll gain the opportunity to continue developing your project alongside Hollywood filmmakers. Exceptional projects may even be acquired for professional film production. Furthermore, top AI filmmakers will have a chance to join Hollywood's world-class studios.
